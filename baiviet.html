Green Nest



The brand name Green Nest represents the industry element and is suggestive of emotions directly to customers, feeling a whole green space covered. Therefore, the logo is designed in the direction of typography, the word is written in sans-serif format to bring neutrality and simplicity. Green Nest aims to be a clean food store, so the stylized G, divided into two branches, shows buds sprouting. Images of leaves from nature bring a sense of calm and harmony with the environment, creating relaxation and freshness. The logo uses green and yellow. Referring to green, people think of life, reproduction, and maturity immediately, promising a generation and a beautiful future that will soon arise. Yellow makes customers think of dynamism, enthusiasm, a creative thought, always turning and changing constantly. Yellow is also the color of sunshine, faith, and strength.
