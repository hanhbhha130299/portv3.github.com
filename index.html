About Me

Bui Hong Hanh



Nice to meet you. My name is Bui Hong Hanh, you can call me Felicia. I’m a student from FPT University majoring in Graphic Design. As a designer, I really like to observe and do a small research about things that I want to create. It really helps me to explore and push my creativity also gives me inspiration. I believe that learning is not only from formal education. You can learn from anything. So, in every work, I tried to figure out what can I learn. I have a lot of interest in Branding and Web Design. I am interested in Japanese and Chinese culture. I love to create something about these two cultures. I also love to play games, read books, go on traveling… Thank you and I hope you are interested in my work.
